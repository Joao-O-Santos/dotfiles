# Skill: copyedit

## Focus Areas

### Titles/Headers
- Punchier/more descriptive alternatives
- Header level issues (section vs subsection)
- Order of sections/subsections

### Structure
- Paragraph flow and transitions
- Topic sentence clarity
- Supporting evidence organization

### Paragraph-Level
- Cohesion and unity
- Logical progression
- Length and pacing

### Sentence-Level
- Clarity and concision
- Word choice and precision
- Grammar and syntax

### Word-Level
- Specific vs vague terms
- Consistent terminology
- Redundancy and filler

### Markdown Style Compliance
- 72-character line wrapping
- Heading spacing
- List formatting
