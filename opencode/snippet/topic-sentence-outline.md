# Examples of Topic-Sentence Outlines

## Example 1: Empirical Paper

- The bias blind spot is well-documented in adults but poorly understood in children.
- Studying development can clarify whether the BBS is distinct from self-enhancement.
- We examine the BBS across childhood, testing its relationship to self-enhancement.
- Results show that the BBS emerges early and increases with age.
- The BBS appears cognitively distinct from other self-enhancing tendencies.

---

## Example 2: Theoretical Paper

- The replication crisis exposed systemic problems in scientific practice.
- Proposed solutions focus on statistics or incentives but ignore philosophical foundations.
- The New Experimentalism offers a framework for understanding scientific progress.
- Applying this framework reveals that the crisis stems from misunderstanding evidence.
- A philosophy-informed approach can guide meaningful reform.

---

**Note:** These examples demonstrate form, style, and structure. Their content and topics are for illustration only—do not copy substance into your writing.
