# Examples of Hamburger Paragraphs

## Example 1: Psychology

The bias blind spot is a robust phenomenon in adult populations. Research shows that people readily identify biases in others while remaining blind to their own (Pronin et al., 2002). This asymmetry persists across domains and is difficult to overcome through training.

---

## Example 2: Biology

Mitochondria are often described as the powerhouses of the cell. They generate ATP through oxidative phosphorylation, providing energy for cellular processes. This function is essential for the survival of most eukaryotic organisms.

---

## Example 3: Economics

Market efficiency assumes that prices reflect all available information. However, behavioral economics demonstrates that cognitive biases systematically distort decision-making (Kahneman & Tversky, 1979). These deviations challenge the rational agent model.
