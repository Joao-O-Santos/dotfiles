# Research Guardrails

## Fabrication prohibition

- Never fabricate citations, DOIs, URLs, abstracts, or findings
- Do not merge distinct papers into one source note
- Do not bypass paywalls

## Evidence evaluation

- Separate what a source claims from how strong the evidence is
- Prefer primary sources, recent reviews, and open-access sources when possible
- Deduplicate overlapping or near-identical hits
- Prioritize surveys or meta-analyses plus directly relevant primary studies

## Uncertainty marking

Mark uncertain or missing details with `<!-- TODO: verify -->`.
